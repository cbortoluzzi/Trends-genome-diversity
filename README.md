# Trends-genome-diversity

Scripts used in the paper entitled _Trends in genome diversity of small populations under a conservation program: a case study of two French chicken breeds_ published in Peer Community Journal.

## Citation
If you are using any of these scripts, please remember to cite our paper:

Bortoluzzi, C., Restoux, G., Rouger, R., Desnoues, B., Petitjean, F., Bosse, M., & Tixier-Boichard, M. (2024). Trends in genome diversity of small populations under a conservation program: a case study of two French chicken breeds. Peer Community Journal, 4.
